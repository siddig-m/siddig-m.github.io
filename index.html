About me

In a world of data, I envision a future where insights drive progress, and I'm excited to be a part of that transformation. My name is Siddig and I’m a recent graduate from Western University where I studied statistics. I’d describe myself as a curious learner, always trying to explore new things through a data-driven approach.

Over the past years, I’ve learned many programming tools and languages while also completing many technical and educational projects in data analytics and machine learning through internships, coursework, and extracurriculars such as Western AI.

Beyond data analysis, I enjoy giving back and making a positive impact in my community. During my time at Western, I exercised this through three years of involvement in Western’s Orientation program as an orientation leader. As an orientation leader, I’ve supported hundreds of incoming students to the university and took on a larger role as the health captain where I planned events and initiatives focused on physical and mental health.

In the future, I’ve made it a mission of mine to couple my interests in driving actionable insights from data in a space where I can continue to help others and contribute to the greater good of the world. I'm excited about the opportunities to create positive change and am always open to new challenges and collaborations.
