About

In this project, we've developed a Social Deprivation Index using data from Chicago, Illinois. This index incorporates five key socio-economic status indicators: Education, Unemployment, Income, Built Environment, and Rent. By normalizing and aggregating these indicators, we've created a comprehensive index that highlights social deprivation patterns in different census tracts across Chicago. This valuable tool provides insights into the social and economic conditions of various areas, aiding in targeted interventions and policy decisions.
